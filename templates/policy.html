Privacy Policy

Last Update : 12 December 2023

Welcome to HireMe, the premier job portal connecting employers and job seekers. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you engage with our website and services.
1. Information We Collect

a. User-Provided Information
When you register for an account on HireMe, we collect essential information such as your name, email address, and other details necessary for creating and managing your profile. Job seekers have the option to provide additional information, including resumes, work experience, education, and skills.

b. Employer Information
Employers provide company details, job listings, and other information essential for recruitment purposes.

c. Automatically Collected Information
We utilize cookies and similar technologies to automatically collect device information, IP addresses, and usage data. This information helps us enhance user experience, analyze trends, and administer the website.

2. How We Use Your Information

a. Job Matching
We leverage the information provided by job seekers to facilitate accurate job matching, connecting them with relevant employment opportunities.

b. Communication
We may use your contact information to communicate with you about your account, job applications, or important service updates. This includes notifications regarding new job listings, application status, and relevant industry news.

c. Improving Services
Your feedback and usage data play a crucial role in enhancing and personalizing our services. We analyze this information to improve user experience, optimize job matching algorithms, and introduce new features.

3. Data Sharing

a. Employers and Job Seekers
Job seeker profiles, excluding contact details, may be shared with employers to facilitate the recruitment process. Employers may contact job seekers directly through our platform.

b. Service Providers
We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in delivering and improving our platform. These providers adhere to strict confidentiality and security standards.

c. Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe that such action is necessary to protect our rights, comply with legal processes, or ensure the safety of our users.

4. Security Measures

a. Data Security
We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

b. Access Controls
Access to your personal information is restricted to authorized personnel who require access to fulfill their job responsibilities.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@hireme.com.

This privacy policy is mentioned for just the project purpose. Any of the policy points mentioned above are not written under legal advisory person.
